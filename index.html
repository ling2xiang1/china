Economic city Shanghai,China

Shanghai,which is one of the largest economic cities in the world,has an are of 6340.5㎢,which about 29 times that of Tokyo. The population in 2019 is very large at 24.28 million. It takes about 4hours by plane from Tokyo.

After the Opium War, it became an important trading port, after which foreign powers invaded.It was released in 1949 and was reborn.It is now the center of economics, finance, trade and nautical industry.

SWU Study Abroad Program

There are two programs for studying abroad in China in Department of International Studies at Showa Women's University.One is a double degree program for two years at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, which is the oldest university in China, and one is language study program for one years at East China Normal University.The former can also be used to obtain a degree.

Learn what

After studying abroad, they will live in a dormitory with other international students. At both universities, students can not only acquire modern Chinese, but also learn about ancient Chinese, Chinese culture and entertainment, and many other fields. There are also many opportunities to interact with students from other country,so we can also learn about multiculturalism.

On non-school days, you can go shopping in the city center, try Shanghai's famous xiaolongbao, get nostalgic in a historic water town, or deepen your knowledge at the Lu Xun Museum, which is probably the most famous museum in China.

Caution

・In many cases, social networking services that can be used in Japan cannot be used in China. ・The water is hard and cannot be drunk directly. ・There are scams that talk to you in Japanese and make you buy expensive things. ・Since shopping with credit cards is common in China, it is easy to get caught with cash.

However, it must be a good opportunity to develop the ability to understand and adapt to different cultures.
